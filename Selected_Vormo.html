VORMO

A risk worth taking for the true daredevil salmon!

About

Are you the adventurous type? Do you like to take risks? The river vormo is filled to the brim with beautiful corals, lots of krill, worms and smaller fish This is has caused salmon to travel here from all over the nordic regions. But: BEWARE! The large average size of salmon in the area has also caused it to be very popular for fishermen and predatory sea creatures waiting for their next salmon. Not for the faint of heart and travel at your own risk!
