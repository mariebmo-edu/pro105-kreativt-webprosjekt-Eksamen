Your next destination is a river known for its big salmon and its beautiful riverbanks.
The river is 103 km long, starting from Karasjok and runs into Porsangerfjorden,
a fjord off the Barents Sea. The area is relatively peaceful and not many reported predators that could potentially harm you. Perfect for the whole family!
